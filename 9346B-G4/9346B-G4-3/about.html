About Us

Camp John Hay is the most popular tourist spot in the City of Pines. It is known for having its wide open area and green landscape making it a great place for relaxation and recreation. Here, people can do activities such as rapelling, picnic, shopping, trailing, and horseback riding. They can also have the tree-top adventure, and golfers can go and play at the golf course. On the other side of Camp John Hay being a tourist attraction, the problem it faces regarding the environment cannot be denied.
Within the past decades until now, environmental problems continue to arise causing natural disasters, health problems, and other forms of destruction. Environment is a part of us, and we, Green Side, intends to educate people and raise awareness about protecting the environment of Camp John Hay.
